Key Terms Every Student Should Know!

Basic Financial Concepts

Understanding how to track income and expenses to create a budget that includes savings for future goals or emergencies. Interest Rates Comprehending how interest rates affect savings and borrowing, including how compound interest works. Inflation Knowing what inflation is and how it reduces purchasing power over time, affecting savings and investment returns

Credit and Debt Management

Learning what a credit score is, how it is calculated, and its impact on the ability to borrow money or secure housing. Types of Debt Understanding different types of debt, including credit card debt, student loans, mortgages, and how to manage them. Responsible Credit Use Knowing how to use credit cards responsibly to avoid high interest and debt accumulation.

Investing and Wealth Building

Grasping the basics of stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and the importance of diversification. Retirement Planning Understanding different retirement accounts, such as 401(k)s and IRAs, and the importance of early and consistent contributions. Risk Management Knowing how to assess and manage risk, including the use of insurance to protect wealth.
